# Food Trendz – Sales Analysis Dashboard
## Project Description
The Food Trendz Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze and visualize sales performance across multiple business dimensions such as campaign, product, customer demographics, and platform. It provides a 360° view of sales metrics, customer behavior, and marketing effectiveness, empowering decision-makers to identify patterns, track KPIs, and optimize sales strategies.

## Key Highlights
- Comprehensive Analysis: Covers campaign performance, buyer composition, and purchase drivers.

- Interactive Visuals: Dynamic tooltips, drill-downs, and filters for in-depth exploration.

- Data Modeling: Built with DAX measures for calculating KPIs like profit, sales, YoY%, and margins.

- Segment-Level Insights: Visual breakdowns by product, region, age group, marital status, and education level.

- Predictive View: Identifies key influencers impacting campaign success and total sales.

- Export Capability: Allows users to export data for deeper offline analysis.

## Key Insights Displayed:

- Top-performing campaigns, products, and sales platforms.

- Correlation between customer demographics (education, age, marital status) and purchase behavior.

- Sales distribution across countries, product types, and platforms (Web, Store, Deals, Catalog).

- Insights into income levels, family size, and website engagement affecting sales.

- Identification of key factors influencing sales such as income range, number of kids/teens at home, and web purchase activity.

## Design Approach:
The dashboard was designed with a focus on clarity, interactivity, and user engagement.

- Data Modeling: All datasets were connected using a star schema to ensure efficient DAX calculations.

- KPIs & Measures: Custom DAX measures were created for key metrics (e.g., Units Sold, Profit, YOY%, Profit Margin).

- UI/UX: Consistent color theme, clear icons, and structured layouts enhance readability.

- Navigation: Tabs and dynamic tooltips guide users through insights effortlessly.

- Performance Optimization: Filters and slicers are optimized to ensure smooth interaction even with large datasets.

## Benefits

- Data-Driven Decisions: Empowers management to track KPIs and identify profitable customer segments.

- Interactivity: Users can filter and drill down to explore specific campaigns or demographics.

- Ease of Use: Intuitive design ensures accessibility for both technical and non-technical users.

- Comprehensive Analysis: Unified view of marketing, sales, and customer data in one dashboard.

- Scalability: Can easily integrate additional datasets (e.g., region, seasonality, promotions).

## Conclusion
The Food Trendz Sales Analysis Dashboard transforms raw sales and campaign data into meaningful insights. Through Power BI’s interactive features, it helps businesses understand customer patterns, identify key influencers, and make informed strategic decisions. It stands as a comprehensive and visually engaging analytics solution for tracking performance and driving growth.
